I am a developer

In 9th grade, my journey into the world of web development began when my father suggested that I give it a try. Intrigued by the idea, I delved into the realm of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, eager to explore this exciting field. What started as a mere suggestion soon turned into a passion that consumed my free time. I spent countless hours tinkering with code, designing websites, and solving puzzles to enhance my skills. Little did I know at the time that this humble beginning would pave the way for a remarkable path ahead.

As my proficiency grew, I sought ways to showcase my progress and creations. I decided to create a CodePen portfolio, which served as a virtual canvas for my projects. With each passing week, my portfolio flourished with dozens of projects, ranging from simple websites to interactive web applications. The platform became an invaluable tool for not only exhibiting my skills but also learning from other talented developers within the community. The feedback and support I received fueled my motivation to keep pushing the boundaries of what I could achieve.

While web development was my initial focus, my curiosity led me to explore other avenues in the realm of programming. I found myself drawn to the world of mobile app development, specifically Android apps, and soon started experimenting with Java and Kotlin. The thrill of creating something tangible that could be used and enjoyed by others was exhilarating. Additionally, I ventured into the realm of video game development, using Unity and C# to bring my imagination to life. These new ventures broadened my skill set and provided me with a deeper understanding of different programming paradigms.

One of the most significant milestones in my journey was the development of a complex website that consumed an entire year of my time and dedication. The project demanded meticulous planning, an eye for design, and an unwavering commitment to delivering a seamless user experience. From wireframing and prototyping to implementing intricate functionalities, every step brought both challenges and triumphs. As the website finally came to life, it stood as a testament to my growth as a developer and my ability to persevere through long-term projects. The satisfaction of seeing users interact with and benefit from the website was immeasurable, making every minute of hard work worthwhile.

About me

As a dedicated student at the prestigious University of Bucharest, Faculty of Mathematics and Informatics, I am immersed in a world of knowledge and intellectual exploration. Being part of this esteemed institution allows me to pursue my passion for mathematics and computer science while learning from some of the brightest minds in the field. The rigorous curriculum and challenging projects have honed my analytical thinking and problem-solving abilities, equipping me with the skills necessary to thrive in the ever-evolving world of technology.

Hailing from Romania, a country rich in history and culture, I was born and currently reside in this beautiful land. Growing up in Romania has given me a deep appreciation for its traditions, folklore, and vibrant landscapes. It is a place where old meets new, where ancient castles coexist with modern cities. The diverse and welcoming nature of Romania has shaped my worldview and fostered a sense of pride in my roots.

Beyond academics, I find solace and inspiration in various hobbies that enrich my life. Playing video games has been a beloved pastime of mine, allowing me to immerse myself in captivating virtual worlds and experience thrilling adventures. Additionally, I have developed a passion for creating digital art, utilizing various software and tools to bring my imagination to life. This form of self-expression allows me to channel my creativity and bring forth visually captivating pieces. When I want to unwind and relax, I often find myself indulging in the world of cinema, exploring a wide range of genres and appreciating the storytelling prowess of talented filmmakers. Movies provide me with a means of escapism and a source of inspiration for my own creative endeavors.
